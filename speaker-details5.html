DB MANIA




This competition involves designing and implementing a database to meet a specific set of requirements.

The winner is determined based on the efficiency and scalability of the database design,as well as the accuracy and completeness of the data stored.

Rules and Regulations of DB mania:

1. Eligibility: The students of Polytechnic community are eligible to participate in this event.

2. Format:

  • Each participant has to perform individually.
  • If the number of participants exceeds the threshold limit, then a screening test will be conducted.
  • The competition will be conducted in 2 or 3 rounds if required.
  • Platform and technology: SQL, Ubuntu OS.
  • The participant has to design a database schema, implement complex queries, or optimize performance.
  • You are forbidden to work with internet, carry mobile phones, external devices, and modify files or directories' permissions in the file system.
3. Time limit: The duration for DB Mania contest will range from 1-2 hours.

4. Judging criteria: The participants will be judged on the following criteriafunctionality, efficiency, and accuracy. The decision of the judges will be final.

5. Prizes: First prize- ₹3000, Second Prize- ₹2000, Third Prize- ₹1000.
