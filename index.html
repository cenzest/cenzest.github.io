GENERAL GUIDELINES

    1. This competition is exclusively for the polytechnic community.The participants should carry college ID proofs at the time of registration.

    2. Participants should follow the rules and regulations of institution.

    3. A candidate is not allowed to participate in multiple events in case of simultaneous events.

    4. All events will coommence at the scheduled time.Punctuality will be appreciated.

    5. Late entries will not be entertained.

    6. Decisions made by the judges and event heads considered as final.

    7. For those from their institutions,the one time registration fee will be 100/-.The participant can participate in multiple events with the one time registration fee.

    8. For RWPCians the one time registration fee will be 30/-.The participant can participate in multiple events with the one time registration fee.

    9. The last date for online registration shall be 23/02/2023,4:00 PM.

    10. Registration fees should be paid at the time of reporting.

    11. If the number of participant for the events exceeds the threshold limit,a screening test will be conducted.

    12. Your data will NOT be shared among any of the sponsors.
