Flag Hunt

An individual competition where participants must find hidden flags using provided hints. Success depends on problem-solving skills and attention to detail.


Rules and Regulations

  • Individual event; team participation is not allowed.
  • A screening test will be conducted if entries exceed the limit.
  • Hints will be provided to locate the flags.
  • Tasks must be completed within the given time.
  • Judges decision will be final.

Prize Details

First  : ₹2000
Second : ₹1500
Third  : ₹1000

Venue : Software Lab 2
